FarmCap

Grow your wealth with passive investments in farming and agrobusiness

Why FarmCap?

Agriculture is a necessity globally and it goes without saying that a country cannot survive without farm yields and agriculture in general.
Unfortunately not enough concern is given to small and average scale farmers, this has led to continuous increase in market price of farm products. I.e. if a farm cannot continue to function and produce for long term, they will eventually shutdown and this will cut down the supply of food to the product market, thereby leading to scarcity of food in homes. Also the lesser the supply, the higher the demand, hence the increase in price of commodity.
FarmCap is here to help everyday citizens and farmers by bridging the 2 sides to solve the problem of lack of proper funding for farmers and the scarcity of food, where farmers get investments from investors(everday citizens) and investors get fresh farm products directly from the source at a lower price.

HOW IT WORKS

FarmCap is a system that connects investors(everyday citizens) to farms nationwide, helping both farmers and investors make profit on the capital. Each investor is paid back its initial capital with interest at end of investment due date (interest rate is set by farms).
Uniquely the investorâ€™s interest will not be paid back in cash, instead the equivalence of the interest in form of agricultural product the farm produces will be given to the investor. i.e. If an investment of N500,000 is made to a rice farm and at the end of the investment period, an interest of 30% (N150,000) is earned, the farm will remit back to the investor its initial capital of N500,000 plus N150,000 worth of rice at market value.
